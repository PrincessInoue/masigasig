Who Are We?

MASIGASIG DISTRIBUTION AND LOGISTICS. INC. (MDLI), is a duly registered corporation which aims to provide fast, reliable and most important affordable distribution and supplychain services. MDLI gives focused support and services to small and medium client companies to address their supply chain needs.

Our Background

Our parent company Masigasig Transport and Logistics Solutions Inc. (MTLSI) was established in 2012 to address the expanding warehousing and transport needs of ETR (an IT providing solutions for sales, inventory, distribution and accounting systems)

MTLSI handles warehousing, delivery and collection of pharmaceutical, cosmetics, other consumer products and medical devices. MTLSI also provides secondary repackaging activities that meet client requirements and industry standards.

In 2021, MTLSI established a subsidiary company, MDLI, to provide focused support and services to small medium client companies to address their supply chain needs.
